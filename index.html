About us

HiLow is a car meet community based around one factor: Family. When you join us, you're joining our family. Our goal is to have a tight-knit feel as a crew, and we've structured the rest of our server around that.
Our main game is GTAV on PC. However, we occasionally do meets in Forza Horizon 4, and we also host events like game nights or racing tournaments. Although structured around cars, we certainly do not see cars as our only common interest. "Cars, Community, Culture."



Cars, Community, Culture

Cars

Although not our only common interest, it sure is the biggest one. Like other groups, meets are one of our primary focuses. However, we do not host daily and do not follow a meet schedule, so keep that in mind.

Community

As mentioned, meets aren't our sole focus. We hold regular events to "spice things up" in addition to meets. We also have a server role system for establishment and player-rankings. In addition to our roles, we also have a server economy which is primarily used for betting in meet events and gambling to your heart's content.

Culture

Finally, culture is what makes us who we are. As noted, we are an extremely limited-capacity and tight-knit crew. We're looking for people who want more than just meets; those who want a serious/realistic yet friendly environment. We don't recommend our crew if you're brand new to meets, but we do not deny people based on experience
